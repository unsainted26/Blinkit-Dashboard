## 5. Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

Quick-commerce companies like Blinkit operate across multiple outlet types, locations, and product categories.
However, without a unified analytical view, it is difficult to answer key questions such as:

* Which outlet types generate the highest revenue?
* How does outlet size or location tier impact sales?
* Which product categories contribute the most to overall revenue?
* How do customer ratings and item visibility affect performance?

---

### Goal of the Dashboard

To provide an **interactive, business-focused analytics tool** that:

* Tracks overall sales performance
* Compares outlet efficiency by type, size, and location
* Identifies top-performing product categories
* Supports strategic decisions in inventory planning, outlet expansion, and pricing
